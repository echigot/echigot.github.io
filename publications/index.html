Publications

Publications in reverse chronological order.

2026

  1. Multi-Objective Optimization for Synthetic-to-Real Style Transfer
    Estelle Chigot, Thomas Oberlin, Manon Huguenin, and Dennis G. Wilson
    In International Conference on the Applications of Evolutionary Computation (EvoStar), 2026
    Abs Bib HTML

    We propose an evolutionary algorithm-based optimization framework for style transfer pipelines applied to synthetic-to-real domain adaptation. The multi-objective formulation allows jointly optimizing visual quality and task performance for runway detection.

  2. Enhancing Runway Detection Models with Synthetic-to-Real Style Transfer
    Estelle Chigot, Solène Barrat, Manon Huguenin, Dennis G. Wilson, and Thomas Oberlin
    Under review, 2026
    Abs Bib

    We apply and benchmark synthetic-to-real style transfer models for runway images under adverse weather conditions, with the goal of enhancing the robustness of operational runway detection models.

2025

  1. Style Transfer with Diffusion Models for Synthetic-to-Real Domain Adaptation
    Estelle Chigot, Dennis G. Wilson, Meriem Ghrib, and Thomas Oberlin
    Computer Vision and Image Understanding, 2025
    Abs Bib HTML

    We propose a diffusion-based style transfer method for synthetic-to-real domain adaptation using segmentation maps, with a focus on adverse weather conditions. The approach bridges the domain gap between synthetic training data and real-world imagery for robust runway detection in degraded conditions.

  2. Synthetic Data for Robust Runway Detection
    Estelle Chigot, Dennis G. Wilson, Meriem Ghrib, Fabrice Jimenez, and Thomas Oberlin
    In International Conference on Computer Analysis of Images and Patterns (CAIP), 2025
    Abs Bib HTML

    We evaluate the impact of simulated data and domain adaptation methods for Airbus runway detection models. Our study benchmarks synthetic-to-real style transfer approaches and quantifies their contribution to model robustness.
