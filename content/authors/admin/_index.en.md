I am an industrial PhD candidate in computer vision at **ISAE-SUPAERO** in collaboration with **Airbus** (CIFRE, 2023–2026). My research focuses on **synthetic-to-real domain adaptation** for robust object recognition, applied to runway detection under adverse weather conditions.

I developed a **diffusion-based style transfer** method and proposed an **evolutionary algorithm** optimisation framework for style transfer pipelines.
